Workflow Garden is the public-facing explanation of the workflow itself. It is where internal process becomes a calm, inspectable learning experience for visitors.

## Why it matters

This project translates repo rules, planning habits, proof expectations, and diary activity into something a non-developer can actually follow.

## What it demonstrates

- archive-style educational design
- content and proof living side by side
- curated diary entries instead of raw change logs
- a visitor-friendly entry point into `repo-bootstrap`, `write-a-prd`, `design-proof`, and `acceptance-proof`
