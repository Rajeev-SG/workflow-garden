`shadcn-ui` appears in the build lane because reliable primitives remove a lot of unnecessary risk. Buttons, sheets, accordions, tabs, and similar controls are easy to underestimate until they are buggy or inaccessible.

The workflow uses solid primitives first so creative effort can go into the part visitors actually feel: hierarchy, narrative, composition, and clarity.
