`frontend-design` is the workflow's push against generic AI-looking composition. It asks for a deliberate visual direction instead of accepting the first safe arrangement of cards and buttons.

For Workflow Garden, that means preserving the archive feel from `Workflow Garden Redesign B` instead of drifting toward a default docs template.
